Core Question

How might psilocybin support recovery by influencing mood, behavior change, and neuroplasticity?

Purpose

This site supports an educational brochure and a growing synthesis of psilocybin's place in recovery research, including neuroplasticity mechanisms, mushroom chemistry, cultural context, and open questions for future study. It is designed as a starting point: simple on the surface, with deeper pages for readers who want the evidence, mechanisms, and context behind the brochure.

Educational note: Personal health context matters. Psychedelics, MAOIs, ibogaine, psychiatric medications, and substance-use treatment decisions are best discussed with qualified clinicians.
